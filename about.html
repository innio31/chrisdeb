About Chrisdeb Academy

A safe, inclusive and stimulating learning environment — building academic excellence, character and confidence from Crèche to Senior Secondary since 2022.

Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

Chrisdeb Academy was founded in 2022 in Olambe, Ogun State, on a simple belief: every child deserves a safe, caring environment where they can be challenged academically and shaped morally at the same time.

What began as a single vision from our founders has grown into a full academy spanning Crèche through Senior Secondary, serving families across Olambe and the wider Ifesowapo community.

Today, Chrisdeb Academy is guided by the same principle that inspired its founding — that excellence and character must grow together, and that a brighter future truly does begin here.

Our Approach

Learning That Grows With Every Child

We combine a strong academic curriculum with innovative, child-centred teaching methods — supported by discipline, prayer, and the effective use of technology in the classroom.

Small class sizes and attentive teachers mean every pupil is known by name, so learning gaps are caught early and every strength is nurtured.

We see parents and the wider community as partners, not spectators, in each child's education — working together to prepare our students for a rapidly changing world.

Where We're Headed

Our Mission & Vision

Two statements that guide every decision we make, in the classroom and beyond.

Mission

At Chrisdeb Academy, our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where every child is empowered to achieve academic excellence, develop strong moral values, and become a confident, responsible, and lifelong learner. We are committed to delivering high-quality education through innovative teaching, character development, discipline, and the effective use of technology, while partnering with parents and the community to prepare our students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Vision

To be a leading centre of excellence in education, recognised for nurturing intellectually competent, morally upright, innovative, and globally competitive learners who positively impact their communities and the world through knowledge, integrity, leadership, and service.
